About Honeypots

Honeypots are security mechanisms designed to detect, deflect, or study unauthorized access attempts to information systems.

  • Choose appropriate port numbers to attract specific types of attacks
  • Use meaningful banners that mimic real services
  • Consider using common usernames and passwords to attract brute force attempts
  • Each honeypot runs in an isolated container for security
